iHelp

Women around the world are attacked everyday, everywhere. We wanted to provide one solution to help these women report incidents, support their needs and minimize future occurances.

There are eighth distinct forms of violence and abuse:


  • Sexual abuse
  • Physical abuse
  • Emotional abuse
  • Psychological
  • Spiritual
  • Cultural
  • Verbal
  • Financial

How to report?

Hotline

An automated phone system which allow discrete reporting and connect to the right NGO/local authorities a mobile app which allow discrete reporting in the following options.

Contact local NGO

Contact local authorities (police force)

Notify support Groups

Support

iHelp users will be allowed to form support groups. They can post discussion/seek help/ask questions and get mentoring.

This will allow women in various ways:

  1. Take preventive measures from any sort of abuse
  2. Recover from post traumatic violent situations

A Database

Created from call/mobile app reporting info to generate useful info (e.g. hot spot)

A map

Will be created identifying this hotspots so women do not venture to these areas and thus prevent further attacks
