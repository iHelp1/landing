iHelp

Women around the world are attacked everyday, everywhere. We wanted to provide one solution to help these women report incidents, support their needs and minimize future occurances.

Studies from different countries show that the percentage of women 15 to 49 years old who've experienced physical and/or sexual violence by an intimate partner in their lifetime ranges from 15% to 70%
